# DRAGON BALL Map Assets

## Background Images
Place in this folder:
- `bg_main.png` — Main background (1600×720)
- `bg_energy_aura.png` — Rotating energy aura/glow overlay (animated effect)

## Platform Images
Place in `./platforms/` folder:
- `plat_ground.png` — Energy floor
- `plat_energy_cube.png` — Energy cube/block platform
- `plat_ki_base.png` — Ki-based platform (semi-transparent)
- `plat_energy_platform.png` — Glowing energy platform
- `plat_energy_nexus.png` — Bright energy nexus (center high platform)

## Music
Place in `../../music/`:
- `dragonball_theme.mp3` — Background music

## Notes
- Backgrounds: 1600×720 pixels
- Platforms: Variable dimensions (check UPGRADE_PLAN.md for specs)
- Format: PNG with transparency support
- Style: Purple (#8B00FF) + Cyan (#00D9FF) color scheme
- Target: < 200KB per image (compress if needed)
